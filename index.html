Welcome to NDHWP

The National Digital Health & Welfare Portal streamlines healthcare and welfare services, allowing patients to view their medical history, receive notifications, and access important documents; doctors to update records and prescribe medications; and administrators to monitor, analyze, and manage system-wide welfare schemes and health data.
